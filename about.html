Team

Josh Levinger is the Commander of Spacedog.xyz, a multi-dimensional digital agency for organizations on a mission. Bridging the worlds of engineering and advocacy, he has a decade of experience building technology for activism. He is a former Director of Technology of the Citizen Engagement Lab, and has degrees in aeronautics and astronautics from MIT and a masters in tools for economic activism from the MIT Media Lab. He used to build robots for the military, but now uses his powers for good.
