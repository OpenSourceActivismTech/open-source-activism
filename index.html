Call Power

Connecting people to their representatives through their phones, CallPower has placed over a million calls to congress since 2015. Built for massive days of action, it is scalable and flexible enough for any campaign, and includes built-in contact data in the US, Canada, and Europe.

Get started now

Text Power

Text messaging flows for campaigns and organizations, building on the HelloVote chatbot which helped registered 160k+ voters in 2016. Design conversational chat experiences with a simple user interface and engage users where they are now.

Check out the beta

Dial Power

Enable campaigns to engage beyond their base with a volunteer-friendly and affordable dialer. Connect enthusiatic callers with a universe of engaged citizens to spread your message, fundraise, or get out the vote.

Why Open Source?

For too long, advocacy tools have been fragmented and siloed. Campaigns and consultants control the code, acting as gatekeepers to innovation and locking up data in proprietary systems.

We think technology should be owned by the movement. Progressives believe we can do more together than we can individually, and that the whole is greater than the sum of its parts. The technology we build and use should reflect the values we advocate.

Our tools should be owned by our movement

Intrigued?

We are looking for developers, designers, user experience advocates, and allied organizations and campaigns to build open tools that push the field forward and are available to all. We have three current tools, and have plans for many more.
